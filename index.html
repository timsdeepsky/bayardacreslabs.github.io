Bayard Acres Labrador Retrievers.

BAYARD ACRES LABRADOR RETRIEVERS 2. Bayard Acres Labrador Retrievers was formed in late 2011 after we moved to an acreage just east of Pella Iowa. Our dream of starting a hobby farm and our daughters desire to own and breed Labrador retrievers has launched us into this new adventure. I am an avid hunter for the past 36 years and know what it is like to hunt behind a great hunting dog. I also know that Labrador retrievers make an awesome family pet. We believe that both can be achieved in the same dog. We are a family owned business that is interested in placing quality Labrador retrievers in a good family environment. We live on an acreage where our dogs have plenty of room to roam and train. Our goal is to provide quality, genetically sound family pets that have great hunting blood lines. Whether you are interested in a quality family pet or a quality hunting dog, our Labrador blood lines will meet these requirements. On Sundays, you may email us but please do not call us as we do not do business on the Lord's day.
